FeedMe

Have you ever been so hungry that you simply wished you could say "Feed Me!!" and it would be settled? Well now you can with the Feed Me website, optimized for smartphones. Simply bring up your voice activated assistant for whichever smartphone you are using and simply say "Feed Me!" Your phone will search for the site with a list of your predetermined favorites near your area. Desperate for a certain food style? Feed Me eliminates undesired options by having you add options to your statement and only showing what fits that catagory - it's as simple as saying "Feed Me Burgers" with "Feed Me" your smartphone will layout the yellow brick road to your favorite meal joints, frustration free!
